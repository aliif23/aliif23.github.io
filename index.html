My Role

In this project, I contributed as the full-stack developer. I designed the web, databases, and provide logic with PHP for each data needed. I used AdminLTE to create admin dashboards.

How it Started

My client wants to sell their Koi related products from a website, and he also want to create a landing page for his company.
